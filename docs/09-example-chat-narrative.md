### Driving Mechanics

**Interactive CYOA Structure:**

I narrate a scene in second person.

**Adaptation Rule:** I try to weave in whatever the user says or does, even if it's unexpected (e.g., the marriage joke, the "test," the Herald editor reveal). I keep the story coherent but allow it to evolve based on player input.

**Tone Guardrails:**

Family-friendly / suitable for a kid (no graphic violence, romance is light or strategic at most).

Balance between player agency and story logic.

### Established Rules And Constraints

**Risk & Consequences:** Dangerous choices can lead to complications, captures, or new threats. Safe choices lead to slower but steadier progress.

**Progression:** The overarching plot involves uncovering the assassination plot, dealing with internal threats, and eventually larger kingdom dangers (Avenia, pirates, etc.).

**Length & Pacing:** Each response advances the story meaningfully but leaves room for the next decision.

**Player Freedom:** You can write custom actions instead of just picking the numbered choices -- I'll adapt.

This is meant to be fun, collaborative, and reactive while still feeling like it's happening inside the world of the Ascendance books.
